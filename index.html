How to Use Porter's Five Forces Analysis

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Click on any of the five force cards to open the analysis modal
  2. Enter your detailed analysis for each force in the text area
  3. Click "Save" to store your analysis (automatically saved to browser)
  4. Repeat for all five forces to complete your analysis

📊 The Five Forces

Potential New Entrants: Analyze barriers to entry, capital requirements, brand loyalty, and regulatory factors that could prevent new competitors from entering your market.

Power of Suppliers: Evaluate supplier concentration, switching costs, availability of substitutes for supplier products, and supplier forward integration threats.

Power of Buyers: Assess buyer concentration, price sensitivity, switching costs, and buyer backward integration potential.

Availability of Substitutes: Identify alternative products or services that could replace your offering and analyze their price-performance trade-offs.

Competition in the Industry: Examine competitor rivalry intensity, industry growth rate, fixed costs, and differentiation levels.

💾 Export & Import

PDF Export: Creates a professional document with the visual diagram and all your analysis content.

JSON Export/Import: Save your analysis data for backup or sharing between devices.

💡 Tips for Better Analysis

  1. Use specific data and examples in your analysis
  2. Consider both current and future trends
  3. Think about how each force impacts profitability
  4. Regular updates as market conditions change
